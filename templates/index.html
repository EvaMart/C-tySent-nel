Welcome to CitySentinel!

A web application developed for the city of Barcelona

City Sentinel is a web-application that displays a map showing the crimes that have taken place in the city of Barcelona based on a database collectively built by the users: victims or witnesses. Its main target audience are people living in and travelling to Barcelona as well as public institutions.

About

CitySentinel is a self-knowledge tool for communities that would provide useful information about safety for all passers-by in the city, no matter if they are tourists or locals.
