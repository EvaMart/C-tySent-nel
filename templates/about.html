About CitySentinel
What is CitySentinel?

CitySentinel is a self-knowledge tool for communities that arose from the need of safety against crimes that occur in the streets of our city, affecting both locals and tourists. The aim of this application is provide fundamental information about the city that wasn't previously available and build a solid database by all citizens that submit their own experiences, which leads to form a solid network against crime.


Notable features
Decisions making Collective intelligence Measurements implementation Technical details
This app help the user in the process of making individuals decisions:

  • Decide the safiest way to go to some place, especially in the night.
  • Avoid hot spots of robberies, street violence and similar crimes.
  • Choose the safiest areas to live in, if you are moving to the city or just looking for accomodation, or if only visiting the city for a while.
This app is focused on the development of a smart community:

  • Powerful database built by crowdsourcing; users submit their own experiences as witnesses or victims.
  • Victims, neighbours and organizations can get in contact with each other, building a strong network against crime.
  • Anonymity. Encouraging users who wish to remain anonymous to report their experience and communicate to those who experienced similar cases.
This app brings valuable information to both citizens and organizations:

  • More police street presence can be implemented in the hot spots of robberies, street violence, etc.
  • Surveillance and track of the effectiveness of the measurements by the citizens.
  • Crime rate can be reduced, both by the prudence of citizens and by the measures implemented
Contents of this app:

  • Map with pins built from information submited by users.
  • Possibility of filter the map pins (by type of crime, time, layer etc.).
  • Dashboard, where the discussion board is implemented and users can get in contact with each other; share experiences, opinions, ask or give support etc.
